Research Areas

1. Advanced Dental Biomaterials

Focus: Development of novel biomaterials incorporating nanodiamonds, zwitterionic compounds, and metal oxide nanoparticles for enhanced mechanical and biological properties.

Key Publications:

  • Nanodiamonds in PMMA-based dental appliances (2019, 2020)
  • Zwitterionic materials for biofilm resistance (2020-2023)
  • Cerium oxide nanozymes for cytoprotective surfaces (2023)

2. 3D-Printed Orthodontic Devices

Focus: Optimization of 3D-printed dental appliances including clear aligners, intraoral appliances, and prosthetic devices.

Key Publications:

  • Mechanical properties of 3D-printed resin appliances (2020)
  • Post-processing of 3D-printed aligners (2024)
  • Surface treatment for biofilm resistance (2024)

3. Orthodontic Technology & Clinical Applications

Focus: Clinical studies on orthodontic devices, CBCT analysis, and digital treatment planning methods.

Key Publications:

  • Miniscrew primary stability studies (2020)
  • CBCT-based asymmetry analysis (2021, 2023)
  • AI-based digital model analysis (2023)

4. Oral Microbiome & Biofilm Studies

Focus: Understanding oral microbial communities and developing materials with enhanced bacterial resistance.

Key Publications:

  • 16S rRNA sequencing of oral microbiome (2022)
  • Multistability in oral microbiota (2023)
  • Antibacterial dental materials (2020-2024)
